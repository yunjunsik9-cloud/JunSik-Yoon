01 — About

Who I Am

I built a YouTube channel from zero — starting as a Jett specialist in Valorant, growing to 14,000 subscribers and accumulating 8.4 million total views. I experimented with League of Legends content, learned what works and what doesn't, and came back with a clearer brand identity.

But as I kept creating, I noticed something I couldn't ignore. English-based channels with similar content were reaching a global audience 10x, 100x larger. Same effort. Completely different scale. That's when it hit me — language isn't just communication. It determines the size of your market.

That realization changed everything. I started studying English seriously, developing a global perspective, and working toward becoming a marketer who can compete in international markets — not just create for one. The WEST program is the next step in that journey.
